About Slay_with_Odeko

Hands you can trust with your hair.

Slay_with_Odeko started with a simple idea: your hair deserves the same care, patience and precision you'd find in a high-end salon, without the commute, the waiting room, or the price tag that comes with it.

Every appointment is one-on-one. No rushing, no shortcuts. Parts are mapped to fit your face, your edges are protected, and your style is tightened just enough to last, never so tight it hurts. Whether you're growing out natural hair, prepping for an event, or just want a fresh look that holds for weeks, you're in good hands.

We come to you, fully equipped, so you can relax at home while we work.

Good to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to buy hair before my appointment?

Yes. All prices on this site cover installation only. Bring your own hair or extensions in the color, length and texture you want, and we'll work with what you have.

How long does an appointment take?

It depends on the style and length you choose. Knotless and Spanish braids in butt length take longer than normal length or crochet. Once you book, we'll confirm an estimated duration on WhatsApp so you can plan your day.

What happens to my KES 500 deposit?

It secures your slot and is paid upfront via M-Pesa. It is non-refundable, and the remaining balance is settled in cash or M-Pesa once your style is finished.

Do you travel to my location?

Yes, Slay_with_Odeko is fully mobile. We bring our own tools and setup to your home, so all you need to provide is a comfortable chair and good lighting.

Can I book outside Monday to Saturday?

We're closed every Sunday to rest. If you have a special occasion that falls on a Sunday, message us on WhatsApp and we'll see what we can do.
